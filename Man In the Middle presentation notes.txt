Man-in-the-middle (MITM)
- Is a cyber attack
- Involves the victim, something they are trying to access and the MITM.

Different Types:
- Wifi Eavesdropping: Hacker mimics wifi connection.
- Email Hijacking: Hacker spoofs email address.

Notable Attacks:
Belkin wireless router incident in 2013
- Take over HTTP connections
- Reroute to Belkin products

Prevention
- Site uses HTTPS
- Check email of sender
- Using firewall
- Using some form of authentication - such as multi form factor
